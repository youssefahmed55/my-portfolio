HomeX Exhibition System

Smart QR-Based Attendance & Registration Platform

Admin Panel

Staff Tool

The Solution

HomeX is a high-performance mobile application designed to simplify exhibition entry flows. It empowers staff to handle thousands of visitors efficiently through an integrated QR scanner and real-time database management.

Key Roles & Features

Admin: Full user directory access, advanced search (QR/Phone/Email), and direct communication with staff via in-app calling.

Employee: Fast on-site registration for new visitors and a simplified attendance marking system to keep queues moving.

Impact

By digitizing the check-in process, HomeX reduces wait times and provides organizers with precise attendance data to measure the success of the event.

Core Stack
